**Document version:** 1

**Project Owner:** Mikhail Korolev (Королёв Михаил Юрьевич), an individual ("Owner")

This Agreement is for corporations and other legal entities whose employees
contribute to the Tessera project. It covers the case where intellectual
property in an employee's contribution belongs to the employer — including
works made in the course of employment duties under Article 1295 of the Civil
Code of the Russian Federation (служебное произведение) or analogous foreign
law.

**How to execute:** This Agreement is executed in writing. Send the completed
and signed Agreement (scan or electronically signed document) to the Owner;
the contact address is listed in CONTRIBUTING.md. The Owner will confirm
receipt and add the designated employees to the CLA allowlist.

## 1. Definitions

"Corporation" means the legal entity executing this Agreement.

"Contribution" means any original work of authorship, including any
modifications or additions to an existing work, that is intentionally
submitted to the Project by an employee of the Corporation designated under
Section 5.
